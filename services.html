IRIS Testing Evaluations

Iris currently offers one service to our customers: fiber test results review and certification. Iris reviews to data and photos taken of the fiber/radio installation to determine if any installations errors exist. This service can performed “real-time” while testing is taking place, or after the testing has been completed. We certify that all the radios are corrected configured and that the RET (remote antenna downtilting capabilities) system is functioning correctly. For these services we currently charge a $1,000.00 fee.
