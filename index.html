Our Mission

Iris exists to serve a wireless construction industry that is facing increasing complexity with regards to technology, customer’s reporting requirements, and financial viability. The wireless industry has evolved from a world where a single frequency was deployed through coaxial cable to a complex system of multiple frequencies operating over a system of hybrid fiber optic cables. Iris exists to ensure that our customers maintain their profit margins while working to deploy today’s complex fiber networks.

Iris is made up of highly experienced business insiders who have access to the very training tools utilized by the world’s largest wireless carriers. The founders of Iris have direct relationships with the industries equipment manufactures. We have acquired the knowledge and developed the relationships required to ensure our customers are not wasting resources on return visits to troubleshoot installation issues. We seek to perform with the greatest efficiency level in the industry. We will provide compensation to our employees that is well above the average. We strive to make all our business decisions based on optimizing our shareholders’ value. We are in business to make the maximum possible profit, we are not in existence to “make the world a better place” through socially progression.
